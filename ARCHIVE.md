## Why Migrated?

### Cost Savings
- **Before**: 14€/month Zone.ee hosting = 168€/year
- **After**: Free GitHub Pages hosting = 0€/year
- **Savings**: 168€/year

- **Before**: Laravel backend (unnecessary complexity)

  - Easy to maintain
  - Perfect for landing page

- ✅ Faster page loads (static files)
- ✅ Better SEO (static HTML)
- ✅ Free CDN (GitHub Pages)
- ✅ Automatic deployments (GitHub Actions)
- ✅ No server maintenance
