## Content Guidelines
- Write for busy parents who scan quickly
- Use clear headings and bullet points
- Include concrete examples and numbers
- Add images to break up text (stored in `/public/images/blog/[post-slug]/`)
- Keep paragraphs short for mobile reading
- End with actionable takeaways when appropriate
